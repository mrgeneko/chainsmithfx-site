Chainsmith FX Support

Guitar effects processor and Neural Amp Modeler host for Mac, iPhone, and iPad.

Contact

For bug reports, feature requests, or any other question about Chainsmith FX, email:

murgencoapps@gmail.com

Please include your device (Mac / iPhone / iPad model), OS version, and the app version (found in the app's About/Settings screen), along with a description of what happened — screenshots or a short screen recording help a lot for anything visual or audio-related.

Reporting an audio issue

If you're hitting a specific audio problem (dropouts, an effect misbehaving, an audio interface not being detected), it helps to note:

  • Which audio interface or device you're using (built-in, USB, aggregate)
  • What was in your signal chain (which effects/nodes, and in what order)
  • Whether it happens consistently or only sometimes
